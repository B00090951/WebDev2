SMO - Social Media Optimization

I have more than a passing interest in SMO. There is no big secret to how to run a successful social media page. The first thing to do is chose a topic that you are interested in. Once you are interest in a topic you can supply the page with endless original content. The second thing would be consistency. You must be consistent with your posts. I would suggest 3 to 4 posts a day. And thirdly I would say know your target market. If you are saying starting a Racing Tip Page on lets say Facebook, well you don’t to target people under the age of 18. Drop me a line and I will analysis your social media and give you an unbiased report.
